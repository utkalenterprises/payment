Payment Terms and Conditions

Welcome to Utkal Enterprises. Before making any payments, please review our terms and conditions thoroughly. By proceeding with payment, you agree to comply with the terms outlined below.

1. Payment Policy: All payments made through our platform are processed securely. Please ensure that you review your invoice and payment details before proceeding.

2. Refunds and Cancellations: Payments made are non-refundable once processed unless specified otherwise in writing by Utkal Enterprises. Cancellation requests must be submitted in writing within 24 hours of the transaction.

3. Credit Line Policy: To qualify for a credit line, customers must complete the onboarding process, which involves filling out our credit line form. Approval is subject to terms and conditions.

4. Interest-Free Period and Late Fees: An interest-free period of 15 days is provided on all outstanding balances. After the 15-day period, a compounding interest rate of 0.5% per day is applied to any outstanding amount.

Example:
If an outstanding amount of ₹1000 remains unpaid after 15 days, the interest will start compounding daily at 0.5%.

Calculation:
Day 1: ₹1000 + 0.5% of ₹1000 = ₹1005
Day 2: ₹1005 + 0.5% of ₹1005 = ₹1010.03
Day 3: ₹1010.03 + 0.5% of ₹1010.03 = ₹1015.08
... and so on.
This shows how quickly the outstanding balance can grow if not settled promptly. It is advisable to clear dues within the interest-free period to avoid additional charges.

5. Contact Us: For any payment-related inquiries or issues, please contact our customer service team at support@utkalenterprises.in.
